What is Hotel Voice AI Agent?
Hotel Voice AI Agent is a 24/7 AI-based voice assistant that allows hotel guests to request services like food, taxi, laundry, and maintenance through natural conversation. It captures voice input, processes it with AI, and triggers automated service delivery.
How does the voice-to-action pipeline work?
The guest speaks naturally to the agent. LiveKit captures the real-time audio, Deepgram converts speech to text, and Phi-3 Mini (LLM) extracts structured data (service type, items, room number). This data is stored in HubSpot CRM, from which background workers dispatch services and process payments via PayU.
Why is HubSpot CRM the central data source?
HubSpot CRM acts as the single source of truth for all guest interactions. Every voice request becomes a structured record. All downstream actions — food dispatch, taxi booking, maintenance assignment, payment processing — are triggered by reading CRM data. This ensures full traceability and audit trails.
